MAINFINEX

United Networks of MAINFINEX

MAINFINEX is a platform with low barriers to entry for flexible, autonomous economies acting together within MAINFINEX’s shared security umbrella. MAINFINEX is a revolution, not just in blockchain technology but also towards enabling fairer peer-to-peer digital jurisdictions.

Providing innovative technology

True interoperability

MAINFINEX enables cross-blockchain transfers of any type of data or asset, not just tokens. Connecting to MAINFINEX gives you the ability to interoperate with a wide variety of blockchains in the MAINFINEX network.

Economic & transactional scalability

MAINFINEX provides unprecedented economic scalability by enabling a common set of validators to secure multiple blockchains. MAINFINEX provides transactional scalability by spreading transactions across multiple parallel blockchains.

Easy blockchain innovation

Create a blockchain in minutes using the blockchain-building framework Substrate. Connect it to MAINFINEX and get interoperability and security from day one. This ease of development helps MAINFINEX's network grow.

Forkless and future-proof

MAINFINEX is designed to be a truly resilient network: it’s able to adapt and upgrade itself without forking. This enables MAINFINEX to easily adapt to changes and upgrade itself as better technologies become available.

Security for everyone

MAINFINEX introduces “pooled security,” where multiple chains are secured by MAINFINEX’s validators. Blockchains can get security from day one when they connect to MAINFINEX, giving them a strong incentive to join the MAINFINEX network.

Network governance driven by users

MAINFINEX has an open governance system. Upgrades to the network are coordinated via on-chain governance, ensuring that MAINFINEX’s development reflects the values of the community and avoids stagnation.

Providing innovative technology

MAINFINEX serves 4 distinct purposes: governance over the network, operation, bonding, and interoperability.

Governance

Polkadot stakeholders have control over the protocol. All privileges, which on other platforms are exclusive to miners, will be given to the relay chain operators (DOT holders). This includes managing exceptional events such as protocol upgrades and fixes.

Bonding

New parachains are added by bonding tokens. Bonded parachains cannot be removed from the network arbitrarily or unbond their DOTs until their lease ends.

Operation

Game theory incentivizes token holders to behave in honest ways. Good actors are rewarded by this mechanism whilst bad actors will lose their stake in the network. This ensures the network stays secure.

Interoperability

For messages to be passed from one blockchain to the next, a fee may be paid by the sender in DOTs. However, this is not required.
